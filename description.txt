Stitcho'mania is a mobile quick reference and tool for knitters featuring several popular stitches. 

Key features of Stitcho'mania:
- Instructions for over 20 stitches.
- Browse by name or by picture, making it easier to find the perfect stitch for any project. 
- Ability to email the pattern to yourself for easier reference away from your mobile device.
- Track multiple projects with our new stitch counter.

Thanks to all who have written reviews and sent us feedback!
